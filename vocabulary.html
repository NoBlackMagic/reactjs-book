Vocabulary

transpiling

Is the act of transforming a language into another language. In our case we write our source files in EC6 and EC7 but Webpack uses a plugin (Babel) to transform them into EC5 so the current browsers can excute the code.

bundling / bundle

Is the act of merging a set of source files into one single (well, it can be more than one) that the browser will execute. We use Webpack to perform this operation and benefit from features like modules loading and transpiling.

"bundle" is the name of the final prouct of the bundling action.
